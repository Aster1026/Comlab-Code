Danganronpa 2: Goodbye Despair
Welcome to Jabberwock Island — a once-idyllic tropical paradise that’s about to turn into the stage
of another twisted class trial. You and your fellow students from Hope’s Peak Academy arrive expecting a carefree school trip, only to find yourselves trapped in a deadly game run by the monochrome bear, Monokuma.
To survive, you’ll need to uncover the truth behind each mysterious murder through investigation and intense courtroom battles. Search for clues, cross-examine your classmates, and expose contradictions before despair consumes you all.
Can hope endure when everyone has a secret to hide?
