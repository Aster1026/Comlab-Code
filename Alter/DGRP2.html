Gift System & Bonds
Outside the deadly trials, hope can still grow — one conversation, one gift at a time.
During free time, you can talk with your fellow Ultimates and offer them presents that match their personal tastes. The right gift deepens your bond and reveals hidden sides of their personalities — fears, dreams, and fragments of their pasts.
Strengthening these friendships isn’t just emotional; it’s strategic. As your relationships grow, you’ll unlock unique skills and abilities that can be used in Class Trials, giving you an edge when logic
clashes with lies.
You could refer to this website to see the your friends' preferences.
Even in despair, connection and hope might be the key to survival.
